FAQ's

How do I post a game?

To post a new game, visit the home page and click the add a game button.

How do I post a comment?

To add a new comment, visit the game post which can be found on the home page.

Do I need a referral?

You will never need a referral to use our website. However, if you would like to refer a friend, feel free to send them this TinyURL: https://tinyurl.com/y2qnq9ku.

Is this free?

You will never need to pay to use our website. Although, you can support us by donating or sponsoring us.

How do I report harassment and discrimination?

We have a zero tolerance policy for harassment and discrimination throughout the platform. If you or someone you know is offended by any comment/message on the website, send us an email and it will be removed ASAP.
