About Among Us Chat


On this site, you are able to create a new game invite, given a Game Name and the Game Code (Private or Public), and to share your thoughts on the game afterwards in the comments. If you run into any problems using our site, please contact us at welcome.amongus@gmail.com


Our mission is to bring Among Us players together, into groups of new friends they just haven't met. Also, to help crewmates catch the imposters, unless you are the imposter in which case, Good Luck!
